The Collaborative Platform

A Connected Ecosystem
Built for Entrepreneurs

Brihatpreneurs is a collaborative platform where entrepreneurs, opportunities, and capital come together to build scalable businesses. The ecosystem is designed to reduce friction, accelerate execution, and enable sustainable growth.

Our Foundation

The Pillars of Success

Five core components working in harmony to transform entrepreneurship in Nepal.

01

Entrepreneurs

At the core of the ecosystem are execution-driven entrepreneurs who build, manage, and scale businesses through active participation and collaboration.

02

Opportunities

The platform provides access to real, revenue-generating opportunities across real estate, property management, retail, service sectors, and brand collaborations.

03

Mentors & Experts

A network of experienced professionals supports entrepreneurs through clinics, advisory sessions, and strategic guidance.

04

Capital & Investment

Entrepreneurs gain access to funding opportunities and financial partnerships that support business expansion and long-term growth.

05

Business Network

The ecosystem connects entrepreneurs with property owners, developers, brands, suppliers, and service providers—creating a strong and collaborative business environment.
