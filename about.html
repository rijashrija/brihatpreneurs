About Brihatpreneurs

Where Entrepreneurs Build and Scale Real Businesses

Brihatpreneurs is a venture-driven ecosystem designed to empower entrepreneurs to build, operate, and scale sustainable businesses through real opportunities, strategic support, and aligned partnerships.

Who We Are

A Curated Ecosystem
Built for Execution.

Brihatpreneurs is not a traditional incubator or coworking space. It is a curated ecosystem built for execution-focused entrepreneurs who are ready to move beyond ideas and focus on building real, revenue-generating businesses.

We work with entrepreneurs across real estate, property management, food and beverage, retail, and service sectors—providing the structure, access, and leverage required to grow and scale.

Our Philosophy

"We believe businesses are built through execution, not just ideas."

The right ecosystem enables entrepreneurs to move faster, make better decisions, and scale efficiently.

Our Vision

To build Nepal’s most trusted entrepreneur-led ecosystem where businesses grow through collaboration, real opportunities, and strong execution.

Our Mission

To empower execution-driven entrepreneurs, unlock access to real business opportunities, enable scalable growth through systems and partnerships, and build sustainable, revenue-generating ventures.

Execution-Ready
